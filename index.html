Who's hiring?

Practix - internship

We are looking for an intern. He will be in charge of building new interactive cross-platform components for TouchDomain, which is developed in .Net.

The internship's goal is building a technology proof-of-concept and to evaluate the person for a further collaboration.

We are looking for a person with knowledge in those fields:
  • Interface development
  • Stand-alone development in .Net and C#
  • Web development in Html5, Javascript, AngularJS (or any other MV* framework)
  • Knowledge of Adobe Illustrator

The internship will be 6 months long in Rovereto. Practix srl is a software company based in Rovereto, in the Meccatronica Hub.

The company is specialised in building interfaces for big screens, interactive totems and interactive tables.
Practix was born from the Centro Ricerche Fondazione Bruno Kessler's ten years experience.
