Thinkinside, a high-tech start-up company based in the Northern part of Italy, providing solutions and technologies for the emerging fields of indoor location-based services and analytics, is seeking a well motivated (i) Frontend Software Developer and (ii) Full Stack Developer, with a proven working experience in the field.
